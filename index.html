About Us

Doxper is used wherever. pen touches paper in healthcare

Better patient care starts with digitisation and documentation.

With our ‘zero behaviour change’ approach, we are seamlessly digitising healthcare data, currently missing in India and other developing economies. Today, we are helping doctors and hospitals digitise to improve their operational, clinical and patient engagement outcomes. In the near future, we believe insights from this rich, longitudinal and demographic specific data will empower providers to optimise processes for the broader healthcare ecosystem.

  • However, clinicians in about 135 countries are still using pen and paper and are in need of a user friendly and cost‐effective entry point for digitisation.

Our Client

Shailesh Prithani

Ceo &Co-Founder

As CEO, Shailesh heads strategy, finance and business development and sets the overall direction & vision for Doxper. Before co-founding Doxper, he led large scale operations, sales organisations, operations support functions & human resources for a decade at Schlumberger. Shailesh earned his bachelor’s degree in computer science engineering from IIT, Roorkee in 2005.

Pawan Jain

Co-Founder & CTO

Pawan. is our Chief Technology Officer, responsible for complete tech, backend architecture, web/mobile app development & hardware-cloud app data integration. He excels in systems architecture and design, systems security, UI and mobile application development. Before Doxper, he worked as lead engineer at Firstrain, Snapstick, and Visible Alpha, and as an architect at Flywheel. Pawan earned his bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering from IIT, Bombay in 2003.

Randeep Singh

Co-Founder & Chief Scientific Officer

As Chief Scientist and primary domain expert, Randeep handles integrated system development, marrying the pen & paper hardware with the machine learning algorithms for handwriting recognition and type texting. His domain expertise in healthcare, research, and precision medicine was acquired through prior work experience at Philips Research, SAP Labs and Tata Memorial centre, a specialist cancer treatment and research centre in India. Randeep earned his bachelor’s and master’s degrees in mechanical engineering in 1998, as well as his Ph.D in IT in 2006, all from IIT, Bombay

Sanjay Bapna

VP, Corporate Sales

Sanjay is a healthcare professional with over 30 years of experience. He specialises in overall business strategy and sales. Prior to joining Doxper, he had a long and successful stint at Philips Healthcare holding leadership positions in sales, business management & country management across India and Southeast Asia. Before Philips, Sanjay had worked with L&T, HCL, HP and Agilent Technologies. Sanjay earned his bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering from NIT, Kurukshetra in 1984.

Parag Agarwal

Head, Partnerships

Parag is a serial entrepreneur with 15 years experience in healthcare innovation across Scotland, India and Southeast Asia. At Doxper, he looks after partnerships and marketing. Prior to Doxper, he was the Co-Founder & CEO of HealthCode.io, a global community of health innovators and influencers. Parag graduated from the University of Strathclyde, Glasgow in 2005.
